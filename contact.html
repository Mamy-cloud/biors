BIORS | Biometry Research and Services is sensitive to privacy issues, and it is important to us to protect the information provided to us. Our privacy and information practices policy inform you about our online information practices, the kinds of information we may collect, how we intend to use and share that information, and how you can correct or change such information.

In order to answer to your information request through the “contact us” page of the site, BIORS | Biometry Research and Services can be led to use your private information (for example, your name and address).
