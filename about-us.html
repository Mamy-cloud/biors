Who we are?

Founded in 2025 and based in Paris area, BIORS | Biometry Research and Services is a collaborative organisation of independent consultants. These experienced consultants work in fundamental and applied statistical research and provide services to Biotech, Pharma, Medical Device Organizations, Cosmetic Industries, Veterinarians and the Food Industry. The team consists of eight biometry experts, including statisticians and statistical programmers, each with an average of 10 years of experience in data analysis for clinical studies across all phases and in various therapeutic areas and a doctor of medicine. They understand clients' needs and deliver robust, tailored solutions across a wide range of life sciences.

Why us ?

Successful deliveries of analysis outputs for a clinical trial depends on a strong biostatistics team, which typically includes a study statistician and a programming team with several statistical programmers. As trials get more complex and biostatistics teams face increased pressure to produce outputs efficiently and on a timely basis, a strong working relationship between the study statistician and the programming team is vital to the success of the analysis project.

We believe that the true power of data lies both in rigorous analysis and technological expertise. That’s why we bring together expert statisticians and passionate programmers to create innovative, tailor-made solutions.

Our multidisciplinary team works hand in hand to transform complex data into clear, actionable insights while developing efficient tools customized to each client’s needs.
